My role in this effort

Although I did not have a specific role within our team, I was more involved in the Figma work during week 2 of the project. Additionally, I contributed to various tasks throughout each phase, which can be viewed in the accompanying FigJam file.

Tools used during this initiative

What did I gain from this project?

I believe it is essential to reflect on the insights gained from a project, regardless of its scale.

  • Insights into InDesign's AI icon maker.
  • A deeper understanding of the method known as "Card Sorting."
  • Insights into how wording significantly impacts communication, depending on the choice of words used.
